Utilizing Mixed Reality to Increase Engagement in Children with Autism

Developing software relevant to aid kids with autism in the realm of shared space mixed reality. Designing a solution to simulate an environment where a kid, under the supervision of the therapist can engage in otherwise dangerous situations to prepare them for real-world experiences. The synchronized environment within Apple Vision Pro (AVP) using SharePlay, enables the therapist to guide the kid throughout the scenario.
